## Spring Data JPA 단점
- JPAs Complexity
- Lazy Loading Exception
- Dirty Checking
- Session / 1st Level Checking
- Proxies for Entities
- Map almost anything to anything

## Spring Data JDBC 설계 디자인
- No Lazy Loading
- No Caching
- No Proxies
- No Deffered flushing
- Very simple & limited & opinionated ORM
